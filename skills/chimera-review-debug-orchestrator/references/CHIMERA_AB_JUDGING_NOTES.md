# Chimera A/B Judging Notes

When comparing two workflow, prompt, or packet variants, judge them on:

- `decision_quality`
  - did the final direction, restraint, or wait call make more sense
- `routing_quality`
  - did the better specialist lanes get used
- `phase_discipline`
  - did the lifecycle avoid illegal promotion
- `uncertainty_honesty`
  - did the run admit stale or weak evidence
- `consumer_usefulness`
  - would Deezoh or an operator understand why the decision happened
- `no_trade_preservation`
  - did restraint survive pressure from narrower bullish or bearish lanes

If the two variants tie mechanically, prefer the one with cleaner causality and better restraint honesty.
