# Chimera Route Overrides

Use these overrides on top of the universal router.

## Choose `reasoning review or council` first when:

- the packet feels wrong even though contracts pass
- Deezoh picked the wrong symbol or wrong side
- no-trade likely should have won
- the user is asking "what did we miss" or "why is this still weak"

## Choose `bundle consumer review` first when:

- the bundle exists but does not help Deezoh decide well
- the problem is clarity, causality, or trader usefulness

## Choose `system replay` first when:

- the issue is phase transition order
- the issue is lifecycle legality
- the issue is "live looked wrong and we need the first divergence quickly"

## Choose `strategy backtest` first when:

- the complaint is signal edge, not workflow quality
- the workflow may be fine but the trade idea itself is weak
