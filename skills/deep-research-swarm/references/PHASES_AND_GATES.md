# Deep Research Swarm Phases And Gates

## Phase Order

1. landscape scan
2. dimension decomposition
3. parallel deep dive
4. cross-verification
5. targeted validation
6. insight extraction
7. writing pipeline

## Gate Questions

### Gate 1: Before Parallel Research

- is the time horizon explicit
- are the dimensions actually different
- do the dimensions still overlap enough for cross-checking

### Gate 2: After Parallel Research

- do all required outputs exist
- are the outputs complete enough to compare
- did workers separate evidence from speculation

### Gate 3: After Cross-Verification

- are high confidence findings explicit
- are conflicts explicit
- is the conflict zone important enough to justify validation

### Gate 4: After Targeted Validation

- were only the failed slices rerun
- did stronger routing resolve the ambiguity
- what still remains unproven

### Gate 5: Before Writing

- do the insights reflect both agreement and conflict
- is the final output shaped for the real user goal
- are action-relevant conclusions separated from weaker findings

## Rerun Rule

Do not restart the whole swarm by default.

Rerun only:

- failed dimensions
- contradictory dimensions
- incomplete synthesis slices

Escalate lane strength only for the rerun slice that actually needs it.
