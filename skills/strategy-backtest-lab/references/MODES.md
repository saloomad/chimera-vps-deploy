# Strategy Backtest Modes

## Quick Matrix

Best for:
- broad scan
- which strategies deserve attention
- which symbol/timeframe pairs look promising

## Deep Review

Best for:
- fewer combinations
- closer walk-forward review
- regime-specific interpretation

## A/B Test

Best for:
- comparing two implementations
- checking whether a new indicator rule improved results
- testing whether an instruction or enforcement change changed strategy outcomes

## Multi-Timeframe Comparison

Best for:
- 15m vs 1h vs 4h vs 1d
- seeing whether a strategy only works on one timeframe
- deciding which timeframe should own thesis vs trigger
